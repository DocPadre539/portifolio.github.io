What I built

I found a chaotic manual workflow for sublimation artwork production. I took initiative, studied VBA for 3 weeks, and built a CorelDRAW automation that assembles layouts, applies customer names/numbers (variable data), and exports ready-to-print files.

Results (real production numbers)

  • 5-shirt setup time: from ~1h40 to ~7 minutes (≈5 min layout + 2 min names/numbers + export)
  • Daily artwork capacity: from max 12 to max 32 arts/day
  • Peak production day: 700+ shirts delivered in a single day (hit weekly + monthly targets)

Reliability

Standardized steps and exports reduced rework and made production consistent under high volume. Today, I also manage the company website and edit commercial videos.
